FUNCTIONS
Volume
  • Move the slider, moving it will change the volume of the player. Defaults to 100%.
More Information
  • Clicking this button will open this page. Includes information on the player, and functions.
Version
  • Set by the developer. Informs user of what version of the player they are running.
Local Time
  • Tells the user what time it is, based off of what their local time zone is by the computer.
Stream
  • Select the station you would like to listen to. No station is selected by default.
